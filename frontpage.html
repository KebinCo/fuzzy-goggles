Contract Mechanics

Contracts are named Ingredient–Region–Settlement Date (e.g., BEEF‑TX‑MAR2026) and settle to official market data. You can place market, limit, or stop orders 24/7.

Contract Specs

Baseline price set at creation; settlement range −50% to +200%; value $0.01–$2.00 based on predicted % change; min 10 contracts, max 10,000 per type.

Pricing

Price = Probability × Payout Multiplier. Example: 40% chance of +25% → trades ≈ $0.40; settles $1.00 if outcome hits.

Leverage

2×–5× for qualified users; margin 20%–50%; auto‑liquidation and position limits with continuous risk monitoring.

Create / Choose a Contract

Select Ingredient‑Region‑Date (e.g., TOMATO‑CA‑JUN2026). Each has a published baseline and specifications.

Trade Continuously

Place market or limit orders. Order book + AMM provide liquidity and real‑time price discovery.

Settle to Data

On the settlement date, contracts auto‑settle to objective data sources (USDA, exchanges, wholesale markets).

How to Trade

Three steps to your first position. Negative balance protection and clear risk controls are always on.

1) Open an Account

Start as Retail (2× max leverage). Upgrade to Active or Professional for analytics, API, and higher limits.

2) Pick a Thesis

Use historical charts, weather, and sentiment to form a probability view (e.g., +25% tomatoes in CA by June).

3) Place Orders

Use market/limit/stop for entries and risk. Track P&L and VaR in real time; set alerts and take‑profit levels.

FAQ

Is this a betting site or a financial market? +
Contracts settle to objective price data, not subjective events. Think of it as a specialized market for food price risk, with strong compliance and user protections.
Who provides liquidity? +
An automated market maker (AMM) plus a public order book. Community liquidity providers can earn fees; the platform bootstraps initial depth.
What about leverage risk? +
Leverage is optional and tiered (2×–5×). Margin requirements, auto‑liquidation, and portfolio limits help manage downside.
How are disputes handled? +
There’s a 48‑hour challenge window with arbitration and an insurance fund for settlement anomalies.
